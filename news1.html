War & Peace

Image Courtesy: Hindustan Times

- Written on August 9, 2024.

Whenever a calamity strikes in a region, the Indian Armed Forces run the rescue operations in full force. They are always ready to serve the country and people without thinking twice. Be it a terror attack or the calamitous circumstances, the Indian Armed Forces always stand firmly by those who need their help.

Motto of the Indian Army is:
"Seva Paramo Dharma"(Service Before Self).

Motto of the Indian Navy is:
"Sam no Varunah"(Be Auspicious unto us Oh Varunah).

Motto of the IAF (Indian Air Force) is:
"Nabham Sparsham Deeptham"(Touch the Sky with Glory).

Many operations have been launched in the past to serve the country. Many of them were security operations such as 'Operation Rakshak', 'Operation Snow Leopard', 'Operation Sarp Vinash', 'Operation All Out', 'Operation Black Tornado' and 'Operation Bandar'. Humanitarian missions include 'Operation Megh Rahat', 'Operation Goodwill' and 'Operation Calm Down'.

Many a times, the Indian Armed Forces also launched operations in other countries like 'Operation Devi Shakti' in Afghanistan, 'Operation Sukoon' during the Lebanon War, 'Operation Sea Waves' during Indian Ocean earthquake, 'Operation Rahat' in Yemen, 'Operation Maitri' in quake-hit Nepal, 'Operation Dost' in Turkey and Syria during earthquakes and 'Operation Ganga' in Ukraine among several others.

Salute to the Indian Armed Forces for bringing smiles to the thousands of crestfallen faces!

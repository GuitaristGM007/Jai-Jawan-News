Media

This is what 'JOURNALISM' should align with while reporting news. Today, entertainment, politics, finance among various vital news beats are profusely on air. Several cartidges are emptied, digital space is utlizied and midnight oils are burnt for having these news penned. However, the limited space dedicated for our military and paramilitary forces could not be justified hitherto.
Jai Jawan News (JJN) cares to weigh in, catering to the niche beat. The intention of the content published is not to cash on the contemporary situations but to inculcate the essence of efforts of our Jawans in the minds of citizens and that, it is the need of the hour to be aware of the workings of our esteemed troops.
We strive to closely examine the articles before publishing. All the information if true, shall be covered reverently and profusely. Inadvertently, if any wrong data is spotted, kindly write to us about it in detail to let us recheck it and avoid any misinformation. We dedicate this humble initiative to the tirelessly aware Jawans, standing tall at the borders, acting as a peacekeeping force during disasters and protecting the overall well-being of India.

Operation Sarp Vinash 2.0 will save the day in J&K

Image Courtesy: AFP Photo

- Written on August 10, 2024.

The Indain Army and the Paramilitary Forces like BSF (Border Security Focre), CRPF (Central Reserve Police Force), ITBP (Indo-Tibetan Border Police) etc. play a pivotal role in curbing insurgency and terrorism along the borders of India. Operation Sarp Vinash was launched in 2003 to wipe out terrorists from J&K. For many years, terrorist groups had been building up their hideouts and bunkers in Poonch region. The Indian Forces killed several terrorists to free the region from terror.

In recent times, the year 2024 again saw the rise in terror attacks in Jammu region. Operation Sarp Vinash has been relaunched to counter the infiltration and menace of terrorism.

The Indian Army along with CRPF, BSF and J&K Police Force is combing thick forests, making good use of drones. The locals are also being made aware about the know-how of tackling the terrorists.

Let us all hope that the bloodshed stops and peace returns to the Valley.
